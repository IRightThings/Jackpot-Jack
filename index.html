How It Works

Our number generator uses a **cryptographically secure random number generator (CSPRNG)** provided by the browser's Web Crypto API. This ensures that the numbers are as unpredictable and truly random as possible, making them ideal for games of chance.

The process for each lottery type involves generating a set of unique random numbers within their specific ranges and then a single special ball number for the Powerball or Mega Millions ball.
